How to make a tissue paper garland

Taylor Larson

About This Page

This is a practice page for class. I will show you how to make a garland out of tissue paper. This is a great way to recycle tissue paper in a neat way. This garland is perfect for those of you who love to diy decor for events and parties.

Supplies you will need

  • Tissue Paper
  • Twine
  • Hot Glue Gun
  • Scissors

Prepare the Tissue Paper

  • Fold tissue paper once hotdog style.
  • Fold once hamburger style.
  • Fold again hotdog style.
  • Cut the tissue paper into thin strips,stopping two inches from the top.
  • Unfold the paper;cut down the middle
  • Unfold the paper again;cut it one more time

How to Make the Tassels

  • Roll each section into strips
  • Twist at the center
  • Twirl across, forming a loop, and glue
  • String finished tassels on twine, and hang
